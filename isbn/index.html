AR for Books

2019.08.27 Dan Lou

Try out the two prototypes that combines web-based AR with ISBN barcode! With the prototypes, one can simply scan the ISBN barcode on a book to get related AR content. To get started, open one of the following webpages in a browser other than IE on your smartphone. Next, scan the ISBN barcode on a book and enjoy the AR magic!

  • Static AR for Books developed with AR.js, A-Frame and QuaggaJS.

  • SLAM AR for Books developed with 8th Wall and QuaggaJS.

The light and platform-neutral AR solutions have benefit of minimum cost on both the development side and the experience side. There is no need to install any additional application on your smartphone to try this out, and it should work with any valid ISBN barcode.
